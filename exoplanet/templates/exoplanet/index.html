Welcome to the Exoplanet Habitability Project!

This app allows you to browse and contribute to the growing field of astrobiology by exploring the properties and potential habitability of exoplanets.

Exoplanets are planets that orbit stars outside our solar system, and studying them can help us understand the diversity of planets and the conditions required for life to exist.

Use the links in the navigation bar to browse existing exoplanets, add new exoplanets to the catalog, or edit the properties of existing exoplanets. Happy exploring!
